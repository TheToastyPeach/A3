John Baskerville

John Baskerville

Errol Morris decided to host a survey in 2012 for The New York Times. After receiving 45,000 responses, it was determined that John Baskerville's 250 - year-old font made people more optimistic when compared to other fonts. This said, the font received criticism close to its creation from opposing designers, stating that the sizing in the typography's curves made it "hurt the eye".
